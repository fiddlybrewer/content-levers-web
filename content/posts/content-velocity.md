Most SEO advice focuses on individual post quality. That matters — but publishing velocity is the multiplier nobody talks about.

## The compounding effect

Each new page is a new entry point from search. Sites that publish consistently build topical authority faster, which lifts all pages — not just the new ones.

## Finding your pace

The right velocity depends on your niche and resources. But the pattern is clear: consistent output beats sporadic perfection every time.

Start with a sustainable cadence. Two posts a week beats ten posts followed by silence.
