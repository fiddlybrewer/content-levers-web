Position #1 isn't what it used to be. SERP features now dominate the fold, and they're available to pages ranking anywhere in the top 10.

## Featured snippets

Structure your content with clear question-and-answer formatting. Use H2s as questions and follow with concise 40-60 word answers.

## People Also Ask

These pull from pages that directly answer related questions. Add an FAQ section to your posts targeting PAA queries.

## The takeaway

Optimizing for SERP features is often easier than climbing from #5 to #1 — and can drive more traffic.
